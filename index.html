About Seneca

Philosophy for modern resilience

Seneca, a Roman Stoic philosopher, teacher, and statesman, offers practical guidance rooted in reason, self-discipline, and moral clarity. This landing page honors his vision by presenting the four virtues that shape a strong life.

Cardinal Virtues

Stoic principles to practice today

🧠

Wisdom

See what is within your control, choose right judgment, and act with clarity. Wisdom turns experience into steady progress.

🛡️

Courage

Face discomfort with calm resolve. Courage helps you move forward when fear would otherwise keep you still.

⚖️

Justice

Act with fairness, generosity, and respect for others. Justice builds trust and honors the shared humanity around you.

🍃

Temperance

Choose balance over impulse. Temperance makes discipline feel empowering rather than restrictive.
